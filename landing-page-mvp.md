## DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Color Scheme
- **Primary**: #3B82F6 (Blue - Trust, professionalism)
- **Secondary**: #10B981 (Green - Success, profit)
- **Accent**: #F59E0B (Amber - Warning, attention)
- **Error**: #EF4444 (Red - Loss, danger)
- **Background**: #FFFFFF (Light mode) / #1F2937 (Dark mode)
- **Text**: #1F2937 (Light mode) / #F9FAFB (Dark mode)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Inter, Outfit, or Poppins (Bold, 600-700 weight)
- **Body**: Inter or Roboto (Regular, 400 weight)
- **Monospace** (for numbers/data): JetBrains Mono or Fira Code

### Spacing & Layout
- **Max Width**: 1280px (centered)
- **Section Padding**: 80px vertical, 24px horizontal
- **Card Spacing**: 24px gap
- **Border Radius**: 8px (cards), 4px (buttons)

### Animations
- **Scroll Animations**: Fade-in, slide-up on scroll
- **Hover Effects**: Scale (1.05), shadow elevation
- **Transitions**: 200-300ms ease-in-out
- **Loading States**: Skeleton screens, spinners

### Responsive Breakpoints
- **Mobile**: < 640px (1 column)
- **Tablet**: 640px - 1024px (2 columns)
- **Desktop**: > 1024px (3-4 columns)

## CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION ELEMENTS

### Above the Fold
- Clear value proposition within 3 seconds
- Prominent CTA button (contrasting color)
- Trust indicators visible immediately

### Throughout Page
- **Sticky CTA**: Floating button on scroll
- **Exit Intent Popup**: Special offer when user tries to leave
- **Live Chat Widget**: Bottom-right corner
- **Progress Indicator**: Show scroll progress

### Forms
- **Email Capture**: Minimal fields (just email for trial)
- **Social Proof**: "X people signed up today"
- **Urgency**: "Limited spots available" or "Offer ends soon"

### Analytics Tracking
- **Events to Track**:
  - CTA clicks
  - Video plays
  - Scroll depth
  - Time on page
  - Form submissions
  - Pricing table interactions

## TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

### Performance
- **Page Load**: < 3 seconds
- **First Contentful Paint**: < 1.5 seconds
- **Images**: WebP format, lazy loading
- **Code**: Minified CSS/JS

### SEO
- **Meta Title**: "Automated Trading Platform | Backtest & Deploy Strategies"
- **Meta Description**: "Professional algorithmic trading platform for retail traders. Backtest strategies, automate execution, and manage risk with our easy-to-use dashboard."
- **Keywords**: algorithmic trading, automated trading, backtesting, trading strategies
- **Schema Markup**: Product, Organization, FAQ

### Accessibility
- **WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance**
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Full support
- **Screen Readers**: Proper ARIA labels
- **Color Contrast**: Minimum 4.5:1 ratio

- Chrome (latest 2 versions)
- Firefox (latest 2 versions)

## CONTENT TONE & VOICE

### Brand Voice
- **Professional**: Trustworthy and credible
- **Approachable**: Not overly technical
- **Empowering**: Focus on user success
- **Transparent**: Honest about risks and limitations

### Writing Guidelines
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences short (< 20 words)
- Avoid jargon unless necessary
- Use numbers and data to build credibility
- Address user pain points directly
- Focus on benefits, not just features
